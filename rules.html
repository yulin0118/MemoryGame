Rules and Diffculty Levels

How To Play

Once you start, click on the buttons in the order you heard them played to you. If you can play all eight notes back in the right order within the oopsie and time limits, you win!

Diffulty Levels

Easy Mode
9 seconds per guess; 2 oopsies allowed; barely speeds up
Medium Mode
7 seconds per guess; 1 oopsie allowed; speeds up a little
Hard Mode
5 seconds per guess; 0 oopsies allowed; speeds up fast

Tips

  • To help your memory, when each botton is played, you will see the musical note that matches its pitch.
  • The game speeds up as you get closer to victory, try to remember the first couple of notes in the beginning so you have less to worry about later.
  • If you make a mistake in a sequence, do not restart the sequence, finish from where you left off.

A Little Challenge

Before you click start, play the sequence [green, green, purple, purple, tan, tan, purple], can you make out what song you just played? Can you try to finish that song?
